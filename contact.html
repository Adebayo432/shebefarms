Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What types of partnerships does SHEBE Farms offer?

+

We welcome distributors, wholesalers, retailers, investors, and agricultural partners. Contact us to discuss how we can collaborate based on your specific interests and goals.

Can I visit the farm?

+

Yes! We welcome farm visits. Please schedule in advance through our contact form or by calling us directly so we can arrange a comprehensive tour of our facilities.

What is your production capacity?

+

We operate on 50 hectares with year-round production capacity using both greenhouse and open-field cultivation. Contact us for specific volume and supply agreement discussions.

How do you ensure quality?

+

We use precision agriculture techniques, sensor-based monitoring, data-driven decisions, and advanced IPM. Every action from planting to harvest is measured and optimized for consistent quality.

What makes SHEBE Farms different?

+

We combine modern technology with sustainable practices. Our data-driven approach, smart farming technology, and commitment to quality set us apart as Nigeria's leading model of modern agriculture.

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

+

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly during business hours.
