About SHEBE FARMS

Where Precision Meets Innovation

Our Journey

Growing Better Crops Using Smarter Systems

Founded with a vision to revolutionize agriculture in Niger State, SHEBE Farms began as a mission to bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and modern agricultural technology.

Established on 50 hectares of fertile land between Minna and Bida in Niger State, we operate the largest commercial crop farm in the region. Our farm combines modern equipment, smart farming tools, data analytics, and responsible land management to produce high-quality, high-yield tomatoes at commercial scale.

Today, we operate one of the most advanced tomato production systems in the region, using a blend of greenhouse structures, open-field cultivation, and technology-informed decision-making.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality crops at commercial scale using technology, innovation, and sustainable practices — while contributing to food security, employment, and agricultural development in Niger State and beyond.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading model of modern, data-driven agriculture, setting new standards for productivity, sustainability, and reliability across the agricultural value chain.

Why SHEBE FARMS

What Sets Us Apart

01

Mechanized Farming

Operating on 50 hectares in Niger State, we utilize advanced machinery for every stage, from land preparation and planting to harvesting and post-harvest handling. Our Ridge Maker, sprayers, transplanter, allow us to boost productivity, reduce labor costs, and maintain consistency across open fields.

02

Smart Farming Technology

Advanced drip irrigation, sensor-based monitoring, GPS-guided machinery, greenhouse climate management, and data-driven crop decisions.

03

Advanced IPM (Integrated Pest Management)

We use traps, scouting, early detection systems, soil-friendly treatments, and minimal chemical intervention for safer, cleaner produce.

04

Sustainability at Scale

Minimal tillage, biochar incorporation, microbes and organic manure application, and efficient water-use through drip lines.
